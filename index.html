Our Story

Lighting the Way Since 2012

From a small workshop to one of the region's most trusted LED signage companies.

LED

Built on Light & Craft

P.K. Signnate LED was founded in 2012 by a team of engineers and designers who believed that a great sign is not just about visibility — it is about identity. Every brand deserves to be seen with clarity and character.

What started as a three-person workshop has grown into a full-service LED signage studio with in-house design, fabrication, and installation teams. We have delivered over 500 projects across 50+ cities.

We invest in the latest LED technology and CNC fabrication equipment so every product we ship meets exacting quality standards — because your sign is the first impression of your brand.

Precision

Every millimetre matters. We cut and mount to exact specification.

Innovation

We adopt new LED technologies before they become mainstream.

Reliability
